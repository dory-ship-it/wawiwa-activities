2 · Fishbone (15 min)

Pick one pain. List two candidate causes under each of six branches, then rank the branches heaviest to lightest. The dominant branch determines the solution category (People means change management; Process means redesign; Tools means automation / document AI; Data means BI / extraction; Policy means push back to the owner; External means a different client conversation).
