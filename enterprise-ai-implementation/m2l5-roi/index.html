The four components (mirror of slide 83)

Quantitative ROI has four parts: cost baseline (hours x headcount x loaded labor + rework + tooling), expected savings (apply a 25-30% demo-to-production buffer), investment (build + license + change management), and payback (the month cumulative savings cross cumulative investment). Lead the executive deck with the payback month, not the lifetime NPV.

Use Microsoft 365 Copilot in Excel to build the model. Show assumptions explicitly; round to defensible numbers.
